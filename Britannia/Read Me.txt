----------------------------------
BRITANNIA 479 - The Winter King
----------------------------------

Britannia 479 - The Winter King is an upcoming Dark Ages CK3 Total Conversion mod for for the "Dark Ages"/post-Roman era of Western Europe, with an initial scope covering sub-Roman Britain and the Anglo-Saxon invasions.
It is partly based on The Warlord Chronicles trilogy by Bernard Cornwell (The Winter King, The Enemy of God, Excalibur), a historical take on the traditional Arthurian legends, and is a port of the CK2 mod.

Manual Installation instructions:
- Unzip the mod into a folder of your choice (Documents\Paradox Interactive\Crusader Kings III\mod is recommended, but not mandatory)
- Copy the Britannia.mod file from the mod folder into Documents\Paradox Interactive\Crusader Kings III\mod.
- Open the Britannia.mod file you just copied into Documents\Paradox Interactive\Crusader Kings III\mod.
- Find the "path=mod/Britannia" at the bottom and replace it with the path of the Britannia folder INSIDE the mod folder. If you unzipped it into the recommended location, this path should be something like "Documents/Paradox Interactive/Crusader Kings III/mod/Britannia/Britannia".
- Select the mod in the launcher and play!

CHANGELOG
---------
=======
Version 0.0.1 (Released XXXX, XX, 2026) - Initial release
- A main campaign scenario starting in 479 AD and extending into the 10th century, focused on the Anglo-Saxon conquest of Britannia and the spread of Christianity
- A completely new map that is significantly more detailed and "zoomed in" than vanilla, with 361 counties covering Britannia south of the Forth
- Comprehensive and era-appropriate cultural dynamic names for most of the major cultures present on the map
- Dynamic culture-based titles and de jure configurations. Britons, Anglo-Saxons, and Romans all have their own fully unique de jure setups, with many more possible variations and flavour decisions
- A new Folkright government form, representing states that are more organized and developed than tribal realms but do not have the larger-scale centralization of feudal or administrative realms
- Mechanics for historical and alt-hist cultural divergences and hybridizations covering the evolution of Briton, Romano-British, and Anglo-Saxon cultures
- A new Spreading of Christianity situation covering the spread of Christianity, allowing you to sponsor missionaries and embrace the religion or alternatively try to stem the tide
- A new Advent of the Saxons struggle covering the Anglo-Saxon invasions of Britannia and its eventual reunification under Anglo-Saxon (or Briton) rule
- A British High Kingship mechanic, allowing a powerful Briton king to take leadership of the many petty kingdoms and pass reforms to slowly reunite the island
- An Anglo-Saxon Bretwalda mechanic, allowing a powerful Anglo-Saxon king to subjugate the other kings and unite the Anglo-Saxons into a single united kingdom
- [maybe] A new Crisis of the 6th Century situation encompassing the Plague of Justinian and the Late Antique Little Ice Age
- Many more smaller features and flavour, including new special buildings, custom MAAs, narratives from the books, new CoAs, and more
